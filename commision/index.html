100% Free!

We represent one of the only - servers that doesn't offer any stores designed to enhance the player's gameplay. This makes us a unique server dedicated to creating an evolving, ambitious and fun game for all Minecraft players.

Transparency and confidentiality!

We take great care with the data we store. We guarantee its security, and no one has access to it. Players can ask to have their data deleted at any time. Thanks to our transparency, we have drawn up a privacy policy and a player respect charter.

Safety!

We are hosted by a trusted company, also used by many companies. What's more, in addition to our hosting provider, we have systems in place to guarantee and reinforce player and server security. We also support TPShield.
